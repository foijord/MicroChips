
Combinations with repetition:     
choose r out of n: order does not matter, you can repeat!

(n + r - 1)! / r!(n - 1)!

level 0: (r = 1, n = 3)
3! / 1! * 2! = 6 / 2 = 3

level 1: (r = 2, n = 3)
4! / 2! * 2! = 24 / 4 = 6

level 2: (r = 3, n = 3)
5! / 3! * 2! = 120 / 12 = 10
